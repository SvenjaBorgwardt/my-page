Kaleido

A tool for giving individual feedback on student writing. Came out of a frustration I had no answer for: with 240 students a week, proper individual feedback was simply not possible.

Students came to me afterwards with questions about their own essays. Not about the mark. About the essay itself.

Local anonymisation → dimension-specific agents → feedback addressed to the student. Output: an HTML page they can read on their phone.

Compass

AI creates a specific failure of self-assessment: the student reads the quality of the output as evidence of their own learning. Illusion of fluency.

Compass fine-tunes a small model so it gives direction, not destination. Not Socratic. Not "here's the fix." Something in between.
