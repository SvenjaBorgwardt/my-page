3. Cookies und Reichweitenmessung (GoatCounter)

GoatCounter setzt keine Cookies und speichert keine Informationen auf Ihrem Endgerät. Ihre IP-Adresse und die vollständige Browser-Kennung (User-Agent) werden nicht gespeichert. Zur Zählung wiederkehrender Besuche wird kurzzeitig ein nicht wiederherstellbarer Zufallswert gebildet, der ausschließlich im Arbeitsspeicher und für höchstens acht Stunden vorgehalten wird. Die Daten werden auf Servern der Hetzner Online GmbH in Finnland und Deutschland (EU) verarbeitet; eine Übermittlung in ein Drittland findet nicht statt. 3. Cookies and analytics (GoatCounter)

This website does not set cookies and does not embed ad networks or social media plugins. For anonymous reach measurement I use GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly analytics tool. The counting script is loaded from the provider's server (gc.zgo.at); in the process your IP address is necessarily transmitted to that server.

GoatCounter sets no cookies and stores no information on your device. It records only aggregated data: the page visited (URL path), the referring page (referrer), browser and operating system, screen width, language, and the country derived from your IP address. Your IP address and the full browser identifier (user agent) are not stored. To count returning visits, a non-recoverable random value is briefly derived and kept only in memory for at most eight hours. Identifying individual persons is therefore practically impossible.

The data is processed on servers of Hetzner Online GmbH in Finland and Germany (EU); no transfer to a third country takes place. The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR; my legitimate interest lies in the statistical analysis of visits to improve this website. Since no cookies are set and no personal data is stored, no consent is required for this; there is therefore no cookie banner. Your right to object under Art. 21 GDPR remains unaffected (section 7). For more information, see GoatCounter's privacy information.

No connection to servers of Google or other font providers is established when you visit this site; your IP address is not transmitted to any third party for this purpose.
